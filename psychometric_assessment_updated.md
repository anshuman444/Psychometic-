No separate age-banded version is required.</li><li>Total items: 132 (22 dimensions × 6 questions each, including the 4 hybrid ability-tested dimensions).</li><li>Estimated completion time: approximately 35–50 minutes in one sitting, based on roughly 15–20 seconds per self-report item and 30–45 seconds per ability-test item.</li><li>A single continuous session is appropriate for this age group. The Scoring Engine should optionally flag (not block) a response set where a student’s direct and reverse-scored answers contradict at the maximum possible margin on 2 or more dimensions, for manual review.</li></ul><p><strong>4.
